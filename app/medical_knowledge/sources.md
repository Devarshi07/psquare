# Medical Knowledge Sources

This file contains citations for all hard-coded medical rules in P Square.

## Blood Pressure

- **ACC/AHA 2017 Guidelines**: <https://www.ahajournals.org/doi/10.1161/HYP.0000000000000065>
- **WHO Global HTN Guidelines 2023**: <https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/9789240081076>
- **Indian Guidelines (ashokahri.org)**: Adapted from ICMR recommendations

## Cholesterol

- **ACC/AHA 2018 Guidelines**: <https://www.ahajournals.org/doi/10.1161/CIR.0000000000000625>
- **NCEP ATP III**: <https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health-topics/all-publications-and-resources/2001-atp-iii-guidelines-prevention>
- **Indian Lipid Guidelines**: Cardiological Society of India (CSI) recommendations

## QRISK3

- **QRISK3 Algorithm**: <https://www.qrisk.org/>
- **QRISK3 2017**: Hippisley-Cox J, et al. Development and validation of QRISK3 risk prediction algorithms. BMJ. 2017;356:j209.
- **Original paper**: <https://www.bmj.com/content/356/bmj.j209>

## Framingham

- **Framingham Risk Score**: <https://www.framinghamheartstudy.org/risk-functions/>
- **D'Agostino 2008**: D'Agostino RB Sr, et al. General cardiovascular risk profile for use in primary care. Circulation. 2008;117(6):743-753.

## Heart Age

- **JBS3 Heart Age**: Joint British Societies' consensus recommendations
- **Implementation**: Based on reverse calculation from QRISK3

## HRV Norms

- **Task Force 1996**: Heart rate variability: standards of measurement, physiological interpretation and clinical use. Circulation. 1996;93(5):1043-1065.
- **Normative values**: U.S. population studies compiled from NHANES

## Indian Thresholds

- **ICMR**: Indian Council of Medical Research guidelines
- **WHO South Asia**: Adapted WHO guidelines for South Asian populations
- **Ethnicity adjustment**: Higher cardiovascular risk at lower BMI/waist values

## Red Flags

- **Heart Attack (FAST)**: American Heart Association
- **Stroke**: National Stroke Association guidelines
- **Hypertensive Crisis**: ACC/AHA emergency thresholds

## BMI/Waist

- **WHO BMI Classification**: <https://www.who.int/news-room/fact-sheets/detail/obesity-and-overweight>
- **Indian BMI**: <https://www.ncdirindia.org/>

## Lp(a)

- **Elevated Lp(a)**: Emerging consensus for >50 mg/dL as elevated
- **South Asian data**: Higher prevalence in Indian populations
